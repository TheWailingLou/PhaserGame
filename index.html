Welcome to R G B!

The point of this game is to collect orbs of different colors, transform into the version of your character, and switch a lever that will help to open a door. Get to the colorful tile at the end of the gold path to complete the level.

Careful though! Switching a lever uses an orb... which can lead to you getting irretrievably stuck, and you'll have to restart the level. For more information on how to play the game, see the tips and tricks (below), or check out the tutorial!

Here are the C O N T R O L S :

Arrow Keys: Movement.
R: Turn into your RED character.
G: Turn into your GREEN character.
B: Turn into your BLUE character.
Space Bar: Reunite all characters at current characters position.
Esc: Bring up in-game menu / restart level.

T I P S & T R I C K S !

-In order to cross red, green, or blue tiles, your character must be the corresponding color. Careful, this is why you get stuck!
-Pressing space is kind of like teleporting. Pretty neat, huh!?
-Switching a lever uses an orb, but you can stay as that color until you press SPACE. Don't press space unless you have to!
-You can't collect an orb when you're red, green, or blue. If you're having trouble collecting an orb, press space!
-If it's raining, bring an umbrella!
